How it works

From first photo to a finished tour

Every property gets the same capture process. Golf courses and schools also get our sponsorship sales and payout model on top.

1

Capture the property

We shoot a full 360° walkthrough — every hole on a course, every hallway on a campus, or the key spaces of a lodge, resort, BnB or filling station.

2

Deliver, or sell sponsorships

Most businesses simply get the finished tour to embed on their site and listings. For golf courses and schools, we also sell individual stops to local sponsors at R4,500 per 6-month cycle.

3

Share the return

On golf courses and schools, every renewal from month seven splits 50/50 — R2,250 to Different Perspective, R2,250 straight to the property.
